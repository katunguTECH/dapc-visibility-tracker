# DAPC Business Visibility Tracker

[![License](https://img.shields.io/badge/license-MIT-green)](LICENSE)

## Overview

The **DAPC Business Visibility Tracker** is a results-driven app designed to help Kenyan businesses measure, understand, and grow their online visibility. The app tracks SEO and AI/GEO performance, local and global exposure, customer leads, and the actions DAPC takes to improve online presence.

> **App Promise:** "Measure your visibility. Track your growth. See how DAPC puts your business on the map."

---

## Features

- **Dashboard**: Shows overall visibility score, breakdown, badges, and growth trends.  
- **Exposure Page**: Tracks local and global search impressions, Google Maps activity, and AI search readiness.  
- **Leads Page**: Tracks phone calls, WhatsApp clicks, form submissions, direction requests, and bookings.  
- **Action Center Page**: Displays tasks DAPC is performing and weekly recommendations.  
- **API Integration**: Fetches live data from internal APIs (currently mocked).
